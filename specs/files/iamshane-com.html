A quick note about comments on posts. The short and long of it is, I’m over comments. They’re a lot of work for very little benefit. In migrating my blog posts to this version of my site, I threw out most of the comments which were total trash or meaningless drivel. I’ve made a few exceptions where I felt like they added some value. Comments are off and are staying off. If you’ve got something to say to me that’s private, email me.
